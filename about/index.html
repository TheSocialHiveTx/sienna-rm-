FOUNDERS

John Isaac “Ike” Epley

Most recently put together the investor group and acquired Datagration Solutions, Inc. (previously named MyrConn Solutions, GmbH) where he is currently the Vice-Chairman of the Board of Directors which is a software platform specializing in the digitization of disparate databases in the oil and gas industry and using machine learning and artificial intelligence to produce more hydrocarbons from the reservoir.

Prior to Datagration, he founded and was Chairman, President, and CEO of Resurgence Resources Group, LLC in January 2017 which was created to take advantage of new technology to complete and refrac oil and gas wells in various basins. Prior to founding he was Managing Director of Deerpath Capital L.P. and Basin Holdings, L.P. where he focused on investments in the oil and gas industry and had an operating role at various times for Basin Holdings.

Prior to his roles at Deerpath and Basin, for 15 years, Ike was Chairman, President, and Chief Executive Officer of Wellogix, Inc., an international software provider for upstream oil and gas operations and leading early technology company in the industry. Prior to these roles, Ike worked in the private equity and investment banking industry.

Ike was educated at The University of Texas, Hendrix College, and The University of Houston. He has been on the board of several operating companies and advised private equity firms on investing in the oil and gas and technology businesses.
