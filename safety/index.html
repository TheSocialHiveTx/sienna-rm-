SAFETY

Our Core Value: Zero Harm

At Citation Fuels, safety is not a "priority" that can be rearranged—it is a core value that dictates every action we take. We operate under a strict "Zero Harm" philosophy, where no task is so urgent that it cannot be done safely. This commitment extends beyond our employees to our contractors, our clients, and the communities where we operate.

Our comprehensive Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) management system is designed to identify, mitigate, and eliminate risks before they result in incidents.

Safety Training

Continuous education for every field operative, ensuring mastery of the latest HSE protocols and emergency response procedures.

HSE Monitoring

Real-time site surveillance and audits to ensure strict adherence to safety plans and regulatory requirements.

Eco Stewardship

Rigorous environmental protections to prevent spills, emissions, and waste, preserving the habitats we work within.

Building a Safety Culture

Safety is a shared responsibility. We empower every individual on our sites with "Stop Work Authority"—the right and duty to cease any activity they believe is unsafe.

  • Job Safety Analysis (JSA): Mandatory daily briefings and risk assessments for every specific field task.
  • Incident Prevention: Root-cause analysis of near-misses to proactively improve site procedures.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Sophisticated response plans and equipment ready for any contingency.
