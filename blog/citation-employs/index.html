A Diverse Workforce for a Changing World

Citation employs a diverse group of individuals that possess different backgrounds, are trained in a wide variety of professional skills and nurture unique talents. We believe that our greatest asset is our collective intelligence and the varied perspectives our team members bring to every project.

In the high-stakes environment of the energy industry, the ability to approach problems from multiple angles is not just an advantage—it's a necessity. From seasoned field veterans with decades of hands-on experience to innovative young engineers pushing the boundaries of technology, Citation's workforce is built on a foundation of mutual respect and shared goals.
