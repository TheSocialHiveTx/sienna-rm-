OUR CULTURE

Operational Excellence & Shared Integrity

The Citation Way

Commitment to operational excellence, integrity, and teamwork are core foundations of the Citation culture and workforce. Citation's people are characterized by a common set of work-oriented skills and a shared sense of purpose that transcends individual roles.

Our culture is defined by transparency and accountability. We believe that by fostering an environment where every voice is heard and every action is purposeful, we can overcome the most complex technological and logistical challenges in the industry.

"Culture is not what we say; it's what we do when no one is watching. At Citation, integrity is our North Star."

Teamwork: The Engine of Success

In the energy sector, no one succeeds alone. Our culture emphasizes cross-disciplinary collaboration, ensuring that engineers, geologists, and procurement specialists work as a single, cohesive unit. This synergy allows us to move faster and react more effectively to market shifts.

Integrity First

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards in every transaction and interaction, from local vendors to global partners.

Service Excellence

Going beyond the baseline requirements to ensure our clients' long-term success and operational stability.

A Shared Commitment to Safety

Our culture is intrinsically linked to our HSE philosophy. We foster an environment where safety is celebrated as a shared success, and risk mitigation is part of every discussion, from the boardroom to the drill site.
